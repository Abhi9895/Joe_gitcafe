# Signature Latte Recipe

## Ingredients:
- 2 shots of espresso
- 1 cup of steamed milk
- 2 tablespoons of vanilla syrup
- Whipped cream (optional)
- Caramel drizzle (optional)

## Instructions:
1. Brew 2 shots of espresso and pour them into a cup.
2. Steam the milk until it reaches a velvety texture.
3. Pour the steamed milk over the espresso shots.
4. Add vanilla syrup and stir well.
5. Top with whipped cream and a drizzle of caramel if desired.
6. Serve hot and enjoy!
